My Projects

The Spectrum Of Emotion: Continued

In this project, I attempt to exand on a previous data analysis project which examined the relationship between song sentiment and the colors of the song's album cover. This version of the project uses a pre-trained sentiment model, and more complex color analysis to further explore a relationship.

The Spectrum of Emotion: Analyzing the Presence of RGB Color Values as a Predictor of Album Sentiment Score

This project uses a sentiment analysis tool to give songs a sort of happiness score. Then, compares the happiness scores across nearly 100 albums with the average color of the album cover.

Boulder's Homelessness Issues

In this project, I compare the available open data with City of Boulder's official statements on homelessness. Boulder aims to bring affordable housing to 15% in the near future, are they on track for that?

Pageviews and Revisions: A Study of Wikipedia's Real-Time Information Flow

Wikipedia is a vast resource of online knowledge that is often changed and reverted. This project examines the real-time information flow regarding the COVID-19 Pandemic as well as the 2023 Hawaii Wildfires as they unfolded.
